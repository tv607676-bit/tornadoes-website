About Tornadoes Academy

Premier Defense Training Center for Overall Development

TORNADOES Academy is a premier defense training center established with the core aim of ensuring the holistic development of children. We organize comprehensive training programs strategically designed to enhance students' physical fitness, mental resilience, social behavior, and instill a profound sense of discipline.

Our curriculum places special focus on fitness training, personality development, online academic learning, and fostering robust teamwork. Under the expert guidance of highly experienced defense trainers, our academy provides an empowering environment that helps children build unwavering confidence and a strong, positive sense of purpose in life.

Our Training Locations

We are currently conducting training programs across multiple locations to serve our communities better, including Kannur, Cheemeni, Mathamangalam, Kunjimangalam, Azhikkal, Chembilode, Koothuparamba, and Iritty.
