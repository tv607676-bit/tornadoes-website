Medical

  • 1

    Candidate must be good in physical and mental health and free from any such disability.

  • 2

    Normal eye sight 6/6 without spectacle or other forms of corrective lenses.

  • 3

    Should have normal hearing with each ear.

  • 4

    Should have sufficient number of natural healthy gum and teeth i.e. minimum 14 dental points.

  • 5

    Candidates with flat foot, knocking knees, Colour blindness, deformity of bones, hydrocele, varicose veins, piles& other deformities will not be accepted for admission.
